  1. Situación inicial 

The Liceo Pedagogico Mentes Creativas school has not had sufficient resources to generate the technological elements that would allow it to expand its learning styles. Despite being a private school, the place where it is located is not of very high strata, so accessing technology has not been an easy path.

However, the school is interested in obtaining more resources that allow it to access new technologies and in this way be able to reach new forms of learning and expand its visibility in the municipality.

Pertinencia Regional

Describa brevemente la manera en la que el proyecto responde a las exigencias y/o necesidades identificadas en el entorno, comunidad, institución, región o territorio que se beneficiará. 

The project responds to the demands and needs of the municipality of Villavicencio, Meta, by addressing key challenges in basic education related to the quality and modernization of the English teaching-learning process. Villavicencio, as a growing city with a strategic role in the region, faces the need to train students with linguistic skills that allow them to access academic and employment opportunities in a globalized context.

 

This project:

 

  • Strengthens bilingual education: By proposing strategies to integrate technology into English classes, it promotes more effective learning, aligned with national bilingualism policies and local development goals.
  • Promotes educational innovation: It responds to the need to modernize pedagogical practices, a priority for institutions in growing municipalities that seek to be at the forefront of education.
  • Promotes social and economic development: By improving the teaching of English, future generations are trained to access better employment and educational opportunities, contributing to the progress of the municipality.
  • Strengthens educational equity: Addresses structural barriers in access to technological resources, a pressing need in Villavicencio, where inequalities in access to educational technologies still persist.

  • Promoting educational quality:

The Mission Unit aims to guarantee high-quality educational processes that respond to the needs of the context. The incorporation of educational technologies is one of the key goals of modern institutions. This project encourages innovation by proposing strategies that combine digital tools with active methodologies, aligning with the mission of promoting transformative educational practices.

Indicadores de Impacto

Defina uno o varios indicadores que den cuenta precisa y medible de los cambios proyectados al comparar la situación inicial de la población beneficiada (línea de base) con la situación deseada al momento de la finalización del proyecto.

 

 

1. Proportion of teachers trained in the use of educational technologies:

- Initial situation: 0% of teachers have received specific training in technology integration.

- Target: At least 80% of English teachers will have participated in a training program on the use of technological tools for teaching.

 

2. Quantity of technological resources acquired and implemented:

- Initial situation: Limited technological resources in the classroom, with less than 2 devices available per classroom.

- Target: 50% increase in the availability of functional technological devices, such as tablets, projectors, and educational software.

 

3. Number of pedagogical strategies that integrate technology:

- Initial situation: Little or no implementation of technology-based methodologies.

- Target: At least 5 new pedagogical strategies based on the use of technology implemented in grades preschool through 5.

 

4. Level of satisfaction of students and teachers regarding the use of technology in classes:

- Initial situation: Low satisfaction due to the lack of technological resources.

- Goal: 30% increase in satisfaction levels measured through surveys applied

before and after the project.

 

5. Visibility of the school in the municipality:

- Initial situation: Low perception of educational innovation in the local community.

- Goal: Participation in at least two local events or initiatives where the technological and methodological advances of the school are presented.

 

6. Academic achievement rate in English:

- Initial situation: Limited average performance levels in English according to internal assessments.

- Goal: 20% increase in the average scores of students' English assessments at the end of the project.

Reflection

During my internship I have been able to learn many interesting things. At first, I didn't understand much and I was afraid of making mistakes, but as the semesters went by I have managed to acquire better teaching skills. This has allowed me to connect better with students and identify their strengths more quickly. On the other hand, this past semester I was able to see how important technology has become in our world. That is why we must make proper use of it in our English classes. In this way we can come up with new strategies for students, taking advantage of the fact that they are now fascinated by technology. In conclusion, I would like to improve and try new technological tools to give my students other visions of learning English, promote what is striking in order to capture their attention by making them part of our technology
